Selected marketing cases

Three cases that show campaign ownership, commercial thinking and measurable outcomes.

PNJ · Acquisition

Scaled acquisition without sacrificing downstream conversion

Challenge

Increase media scale while maintaining inquiry quality and store-level conversion.

Approach

Balanced awareness, engagement, traffic and conversion activity using creative saturation, response quality and sales feedback.

Result: 3.0–4.25x more new inquiries at comparable spend, conversion doubled to ~40%, estimated converted-customer cost down 83–88%.

An Duy Jewelry · Reconciled attribution

Built a repeatable Meta Ads revenue engine for An Duy Jewelry

Challenge

Grow direct-to-consumer demand for a retail brand developed from a wholesale jewelry group.

Approach

Continuous product, audience, format and creative testing, supported by daily sales updates and monthly accounting reconciliation.

Result: VND 240–260M (≈ USD 9.1–9.9K) monthly ad-attributed revenue from VND 57–65M (≈ USD 2.2–2.5K) media spend, equivalent to 3.7–4.6x reconciled ROAS.

Asana Pearl Jewelry · Live commerce

Used paid live-stream acquisition to support consistent monthly revenue for Asana Pearl Jewelry

Challenge

Maintain audience flow and sales efficiency across recurring Facebook Live sessions.

Approach

Optimized live-ad pacing, audience selection, product priority and performance based on engagement and closing feedback.

Result: VND 380–410M (≈ USD 14.4–15.6K) monthly total revenue supported, with approximately 80% attributed to paid live-stream acquisition.

Selected operational analytics applications

Budget planning & variance

Connected annual plans, pacing and actual performance

Business problem

Leadership needed channel, brand, product and monthly visibility across expanding marketing investment.

Analysis

Built Excel/Google Sheets planning structures for assumptions, actual-versus-plan, variance, pacing and rolling reforecast.

Decision output: clearer allocation logic across VND 3.3B (≈ USD 125K, 2024), VND 4.5B (≈ USD 171K, 2025) digital plans and exposure to VND 40B (≈ USD 1.52M) planned 2026 company-wide marketing investment. Within the FMCG role, also structured a VND 362.5M (≈ USD 13.8K) TikTok Code Ads allocation plan by brand, product priority, reach target and budget weight.

Revenue attribution

Reconciled advertising activity with sales and accounting data

Business problem

Platform metrics alone could not confirm how much revenue was actually generated by paid media.

Analysis

Compared daily campaign activity with order and sales records, then used the client’s monthly accounting reconciliation as the final revenue validation layer.

Decision output: defensible monthly ad-attributed revenue and 3.7–4.6x reconciled ROAS for An Duy Jewelry.

Funnel diagnostics

Separated media efficiency from sales-conversion constraints

Business problem

A campaign could generate engagement or inquiries but still fail to create purchases.

Analysis

Connected reach, CTR, traffic, inquiry volume, response quality and purchase conversion to identify the true bottleneck.

Decision output: targeted creative, audience and budget reallocations while distinguishing platform issues from sales follow-up issues.
