Jewelry · Domestic performance

Stable scaling toward VND 400M monthly spend

Challenge

Increase media volume without allowing acquisition costs to rise or weakening product sell-through.

Approach

Rebalanced reach, engagement, traffic and conversion campaigns using saturation, creative performance, response quality and sales conversion data.

Challenge

Expand brand and product coverage while protecting domestic efficiency and supporting overseas market development.

Approach

Compared prior-year actuals, rebuilt KPI assumptions, removed low-value follower objectives, rebalanced channel weights and added market-specific awareness activity.

Result: A repeatable annual planning model with explicit KPI, budget, variance and brand/product allocation logic across Meta, Google, YouTube, TikTok and Zalo.
